Publish stats for visitors

Your data here is only saved in this browser — visitors to your stats page can't see it. To make it public, download the publish file below and upload it to your website in the same folder as solar-stats.html, replacing any existing copy. Do this any time you want to push an update.

The file must be named exactly solar-stats-data.json and sit next to solar-stats.html on your web host. Upload via your site's file manager, cPanel, FTP client, or however you normally update your site files.

Data management

This is a personal backup of your data — separate from publishing. Data is stored in your browser's localStorage, so export a backup regularly and import it on any other device or after clearing your browser.
