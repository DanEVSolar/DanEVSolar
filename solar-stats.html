Scenario assumptions
Where a month has an actual grid import figure entered, that's used directly instead of any estimate below — always more accurate.
Flexible, no solar: No battery. Home + EV consumption imported at the flat day rate.
Flexible, with solar+battery: Uses actual grid import if entered; otherwise estimated as the shortfall between self-consumption and an assumed typical daily demand (≈ battery capacity, or panel capacity × 3). Imported at the flat day rate — no cheap window on Flexible.
Int. Go, no solar, no battery: EV + 25% of home at off-peak; 75% of home at day rate.
Int. Go, with solar+battery: Uses actual grid import if entered, assumed off-peak. Otherwise modelled as battery charged fully overnight at off-peak, covering all daytime home load, plus EV at off-peak. Solar exported at full SEG rate.
